# Support

<p class="meta">Just One Tap · iOS app</p>

<p>Need help with Just One Tap? Find answers below.</p>

<h2>How to use the app</h2>
<p><strong>Solo</strong> — press and hold the "TAP" button while speaking. Release to translate.</p>
<p><strong>Duo</strong> — tap the "TAP!" button on either half to start a face-to-face conversation.</p>
<p><strong>Camera</strong> — point your camera at any printed text, then tap the round button to capture and translate.</p>
<p><strong>Text</strong> — type or paste text in the bottom box; the translation appears in the top box.</p>

<h2>Switch languages</h2>
<p>Tap any flag at the top to change the source or target language. The app supports 24 languages, all working in every mode.</p>

<h2>Translation requires internet?</h2>
<p>Only the first time you use a language pair. The app downloads a small offline model (~30 MB) once. After that, all translations work fully offline.</p>

<h2>The app does not recognize my voice</h2>
<p>Make sure microphone permission is granted in iOS Settings &gt; Just One Tap. Speak clearly in the source language selected at the top.</p>

<h2>Camera does not detect text</h2>
<p>Hold the camera steady, ensure good lighting, and that the text is in the source language selected. Tap the round button to capture.</p>

<h2>Technology — how it works</h2>
<p>Just One Tap runs entirely on your iPhone using Apple frameworks and Google's on-device ML Kit. Nothing goes to a remote server.</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Camera OCR</strong> — Apple Vision Framework (VNRecognizeTextRequest)</li>
<li><strong>Voice recognition</strong> — iOS Speech Recognition (SFSpeechRecognizer, on-device only)</li>
<li><strong>Text-to-speech</strong> — Apple AVSpeechSynthesizer</li>
<li><strong>Translation</strong> — Google ML Kit Translate, on-device (Apple Neural Engine accelerated)</li>
</ul>
<p>No cloud servers, no data uploaded, no analytics. Internet is only used the first time you use a language pair to download a small offline model (~30 MB).</p>

<h2>Privacy</h2>
<p>Just One Tap collects no data. Voice, photos, and text never leave your iPhone. See our <a href="./legal.html#privacy">Privacy Policy</a>.</p>

<h2>Refunds & purchase</h2>
<p>Refunds are handled directly by Apple per their App Store refund policy. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com.</p>
