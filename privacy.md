# Privacy Policy

<p class="meta">Last updated · May 6, 2026</p>

<p>Just One Tap is a privacy-first translation app. <strong>All processing happens on your device.</strong> No voice, image, or text data is sent to any server. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.</p>

<h2>Data we do NOT collect</h2>
<p>Voice recordings · Photos taken with the camera · Text you type or translate · Your location · Your contacts · Any usage analytics or tracking · Your Apple ID or any account info.</p>

<h2>How translation works</h2>
<p><strong>Speech-to-Text</strong> — voice processed locally on iPhone via Apple's on-device speech recognition. Audio never leaves your device.<br/>
<strong>Translation</strong> — Google ML Kit Translate, on-device library. First use of a language pair downloads a small model (~30 MB) once. After that, all translations run offline on your Neural Engine. The text you translate is never sent anywhere.<br/>
<strong>OCR (Camera)</strong> — image text recognition runs fully on-device. Photos are processed locally and never uploaded.<br/>
<strong>Text-to-Speech</strong> — Apple's on-device speech synthesis. No audio is sent to any server.</p>

<h2>Network usage</h2>
<p>Internet is required only for the first download of language translation models (~30 MB per language pair, one-time) and optional over-the-air app updates. After initial setup, the app works fully offline.</p>

<h2>Children's privacy</h2>
<p>Just One Tap does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.</p>

<h2>Changes to this policy</h2>
<p>We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted with a new "Last updated" date.</p>

<h2>Contact</h2>
<p>For privacy questions, please use the support link on the Just One Tap App Store page.</p>

<small><strong>Third-party services</strong> — Apple iOS frameworks (Speech Recognition, AVSpeechSynthesizer, Vision) process on-device under Apple's privacy policy. Google ML Kit (Translate, Text Recognition) processes on-device. Expo Updates pushes small JavaScript updates to the app; no personal data collected.</small>
