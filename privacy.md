# Privacy Policy — Just One Tap

**Last updated: May 6, 2026**

---

## Summary

Just One Tap is a privacy-first translation app. **All processing happens on your device.** No voice, image, or text data is sent to any server. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

---

## Data we do NOT collect

- Your voice recordings
- Photos taken with the camera
- Text you type or translate
- Your location
- Your contacts
- Any usage analytics or tracking
- Your Apple ID or any account info

---

## How translation works

When you use Just One Tap:

1. **Speech-to-Text (STT)** — Your voice is processed locally on your iPhone using Apple's on-device speech recognition. The audio never leaves your device.
2. **Translation** — Google ML Kit Translate, an on-device library. The first time you use a language pair, a small model file (~30 MB) is downloaded once. After that, all translations run offline on your device's Neural Engine. The text you translate is never sent anywhere.
3. **OCR (Camera)** — Image text recognition runs fully on-device. Photos are processed locally and never uploaded.
4. **Text-to-Speech (TTS)** — Apple's on-device speech synthesis is used. No audio is sent to any server.

---

## Network usage

Just One Tap requires internet only for:

- The first download of language translation models (~30 MB per language pair, one-time)
- Optional over-the-air (OTA) app updates

After initial setup, the app works fully offline.

---

## Children's privacy

Just One Tap does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.
